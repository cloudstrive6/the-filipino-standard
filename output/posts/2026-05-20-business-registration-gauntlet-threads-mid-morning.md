Starting a small business in the Philippines can take 13 separate steps.

SEC or DTI. BIR. Barangay. Mayor's permit. Sanitary. Fire safety. Weeks of running between offices.

Every step is a fee. Every fee is a small kingdom.

The friction is not a glitch. It is the design. Remove it, and a lot of people lose their cut.

Dapat idemand natin ang gobyernong gumagana.
