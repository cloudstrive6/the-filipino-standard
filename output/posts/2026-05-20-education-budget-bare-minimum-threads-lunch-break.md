# Threads post (publish-ready)

After 30 years, the education budget finally hit the global minimum. The government is calling it historic.

UNESCO set 4% of GDP as the floor, not the ceiling. The recommendation is 4 to 6%. For three decades the country sat below it, averaging 3.2%.

Reaching the bare minimum is not a milestone. Hindi tayo dapat sanay sa mediocre. It is an overdue bill.
