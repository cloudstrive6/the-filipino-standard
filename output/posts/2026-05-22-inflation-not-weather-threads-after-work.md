# Full Threads post (publish-ready)

Hindi 'yan bagyo.

Prices in April rose 7.2 percent, the fastest in over three years. Transport alone jumped 21.4 percent. Filipinos are told to adjust, to be resilient, to hope next month is kinder.

But inflation is not weather. It is the outcome of choices about fuel, wages, and who the economy is built to protect.

A typhoon, you survive. This, a government is supposed to prevent.
