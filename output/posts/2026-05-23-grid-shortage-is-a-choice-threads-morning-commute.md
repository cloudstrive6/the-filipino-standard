# Full Threads post (publish-ready)

Summer comes on schedule every year. The power supply never does.

This month, Luzon and the Visayas have bounced between yellow and red alerts. Rotating brownouts. Plants on forced outage. Reserves too thin to survive one hot afternoon.

Alam na natin 'to, taon-taon.

A shortage the calendar warns you about a year in advance is not an emergency. It is a choice not to prepare.
