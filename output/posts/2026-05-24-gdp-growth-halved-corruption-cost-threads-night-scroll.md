Part of it is an oil shock abroad. No country controls a war in the Middle East.

The rest is a flood-control corruption scandal. Money meant to shield Filipinos from floods, gone.

Corruption is not an abstraction. It shows up in the GDP print. It is the difference between a country that grows and one that stalls.
