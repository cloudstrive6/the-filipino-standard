# Threads Post: The Grid Acts Surprised Every Summer

**Hook:** The heat wave was forecast weeks ago. The brownouts that came with it should surprise no one.

## Caption (verbatim, ready to publish)

The heat wave was forecast weeks ago. The brownouts that came with it should surprise no one.

Every summer, the same script: yellow and red grid alerts, rotating brownouts, power plants on forced outage while demand peaks. This May, the Visayas grid hit 10 yellow alerts in 11 days.

Bakit tayo laging nagugulat?

Summer never moves on the calendar. A grid that buckles every time it arrives is not unlucky.

The heat is not the emergency. The thin reserves are a choice.
