# Threads Post: World's Heaviest Users, Captive Market

**Hook:** Filipinos spend more time on social media than almost anyone on earth. (Recognition + number hook, then the duopoly turn.)

## Caption (verbatim, ready to publish)

Filipinos spend more time on social media than almost anyone on earth. Second only to Kenya. Nearly 5 hours a day.

So who serves all that time online? Dalawa lang ang naghahari. PLDT and Globe still corner a market the World Bank itself called a duopoly.

Premium prices. Middling speed. No real pressure to improve.

The most online people on earth, paying more for less.
