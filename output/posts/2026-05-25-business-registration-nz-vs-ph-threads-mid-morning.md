Hindi tayo dapat sanay sa mediocre.

In New Zealand, registering a business takes about an hour. One form, online, around 130 dollars, done before the coffee cools.

In the Philippines: SEC, BIR, barangay, mayor's permit, fire, sanitary. Thirteen-plus steps. Weeks of queues.

It's not perfect, no country is. But the floor is a choice, and Filipinos were taught to call the wait normal.
