Over half a trillion pesos. That is what flood control has cost Filipinos since 2022.

Almost 10,000 projects. The floods never stopped.

Hindi ito aksidente. Sinadya ito.

Fifteen contractors took more than a hundred billion of it. The same streets still drown every rainy season, the same families still bailing out their homes.

The Constitution calls protecting the people the government's prime duty. Half a trillion pesos, and the water still wins.
