Transparency keeps getting promised like it's a gift the government might finally hand down.

It has been the law since 1987.

Article II, Section 28 of the Constitution: "full public disclosure of all its transactions involving public interest."

Thirty-nine years of that command on paper. Thirty-nine years of Filipinos still asking where the money went.

Kailan pa ba?
